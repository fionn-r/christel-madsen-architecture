As an aspiring architect currently studying at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts, School of Architecture, I am passionate about creating sustainable and socially responsible designs that positively impact both the environment and society. With a keen interest in the environmental impact of architecture, I am committed to implementing sustainable practices and materials into my designs in order to reduce the carbon footprint of the built environment. Additionally, I am deeply invested in exploring how architecture can contribute to the wellbeing and development of local communities. Through my studies, I have developed a strong set of skills in design, drafting, and project management, and am excited to apply these skills to create innovative, sustainable, and socially responsible architecture.
